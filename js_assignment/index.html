Instructions:

The game is played between you (the player) and the computer. In each round, both you and the computer draw a card from your respective decks. The player with the higher card wins the round and earns a point.

Special Tie Rule: If both players draw the same card, it results in a "war." In this case, both you and the computer will each select one of three new cards. The player with the highest card from the three chosen will win all the points from the round (1 point + 3 points for the war).

If another tie occurs during the war, all cards from that round are discarded, and no points will be awarded.

The game continues for several rounds, and at the end, the player with the most points wins the game. Good luck!
